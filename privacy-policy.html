Privacy Policy for Kao Thai Food Truck

At Kao Thai Food Truck, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

Collection of Personal Information

We collect personal information, such as your name, email address, and phone number, when you submit a contact form on our website. This information is collected in order to respond to your inquiries and provide information about our services.

Collection Method

The personal information is collected through the contact form on our website.

Purpose of Collection

The personal information that we collect is used solely for the purpose of responding to your inquiries and providing information about our services.

Sharing of Personal Information

We do not share your personal information with any third parties.

Storage of Personal Information

Your personal information is stored in accordance with industry-standard security protocols on getform.io.

Retention of Personal Information

We retain your personal information for a maximum period of 1 year from the date of submission.

User Rights

You have the right to request that we delete your personal information at any time. To make such a request, please email us at eat@kaothai.ie.

Marketing Communications

We do not send marketing communications.

Cookies

We use cookies on our website to help us understand how users interact with our site and improve your experience. These cookies are created by Google Analytics.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy or our handling of your personal information, please contact us at eat@kaothai.ie.

Legal Basis for Processing Personal Information

We rely on legitimate interests as the legal basis for processing personal information.

Updates to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify or update this Privacy Policy at any time without prior notice. Any changes will be posted on our website with the date of the latest revision.
