Careers

Job Title: Food Truck Chef (Fixed Location)

Company Description

Kao Thai is a vibrant food truck dedicated to bringing the authentic flavors of Thailand and other Asian cuisines to the community. Located at Elmgrove Flower Farm in Gormanstown, Co. Meath, we pride ourselves on our commitment to freshness, quality, and exceptional customer service. Our team is passionate about creating memorable dining experiences through delicious street food.

Responsibilities

  • Prepare and cook food to order, adhering to customer dietary requests and specifications.
  • Maintain cleanliness and hygiene within the food truck at all times.
  • Take and manage customer orders with professionalism and efficiency.
  • Ensure compliance with food safety and health regulations.
  • Collaborate with the team to maintain high standards of food quality and service.

Requirements

  • Experience: Prior experience working in a kitchen is required; training will be provided.
  • Skills: Experience in Thai cuisine is preferred. Fluent English is essential for effective customer interaction.

Work Schedule

Flexible part-time or full-time options, up to 40 hours per week. Operating hours: Wednesday to Sunday, 12:00 pm to 10:00 pm.

Compensation

€13 - €16 per hour, depending on experience.

Location

Elmgrove Flower Farm, Gormanstown, Co. Meath.

Company Culture

At Kao Thai, we are dedicated to providing fresh and delicious street food, specializing in Thai and other Asian cuisines. We pride ourselves on a customer-focused approach and a friendly, team-oriented environment.

Additional Perks

  • Free parking.
  • Free meals during shifts.

Application Process

We are hiring immediately. Interested applicants should send their CV to eat@kaothai.ie or deliver it in person.

Join us at Kao Thai and be part of an exciting and dynamic food truck team serving high-quality Asian cuisine!
