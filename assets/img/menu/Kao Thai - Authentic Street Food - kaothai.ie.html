Pad Thai

Thai Green Curry

Fresh mixed peppers, onions, basil leaves, all simmered in a lively green curry sauce with your choice of protein.

Thai Red Curry

Fresh mixed peppers, onions, basil leaves, all simmered in a rich and aromatic red curry sauce with your choice of protein.

Thai Yellow Curry

Fresh mixed peppers, onions, basil leaves, all simmered in a creamy, fragrant yellow curry sauce with your choice of protein.

Thai Fried Rice

Jasmine rice stir-fried with broccoli, carrots and onions, your choice of protein, and our special blend of seasonings garnished with spring onions.

Mixed fresh vegetables stir-fried with fresh basil, all enveloped in a fiery yet balanced chili and garlic sauce with your choice of protein and fried egg.

Pad See Ew
